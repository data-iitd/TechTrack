Install PC Anywhere
https://www.wikihow.com/Install-PC-Anywhere

0 - Steps

1 - Review the computer system requirements.
Review the computer system requirements. Check the system requirements for each computer before you install PC Anywhere to ensure compatibility.
- Some minimum requirements include having Windows 98, Mac OS 10.4.3 or later versions of these. The product also recommends having Internet Explorer 6, Firefox 1.5 or later versions of these.

2 - Choose the installation options.
Choose the installation options. Pick the full-system installation or customize your software installation. Full-system installation comes with all the features, tools and functions, while the customized option allows you to choose the specific features that you want.

3 - Scan for earlier versions of the software.
Scan for earlier versions of the software. PC Anywhere automatically checks your computer(s) for earlier versions of its software.
- If you have an earlier version of PC Anywhere, then you may choose to keep saved configuration data and install the new software.
- Uninstall PC Anywhere versions older than 10.x before installing the upgraded version. You cannot save versions of PC Anywhere prior to 10.x.

4 - Place the product CD into your computer's CD_DVD-ROM drive and wait for the PC Anywhere window to appear on the screen.
Place the product CD into your computer's CD/DVD-ROM drive and wait for the PC Anywhere window to appear on the screen. Click "Install Symantec PCAnywhere," then "Next." Two setup panels will appear on the screen: "Destination Folder" and "Custom Setup."
- Choose "Next" to install the software in the "Destination Folder." If you prefer to specify the folder where you want to store the program, choose "Custom Setup" before hitting "Next."

5 - Go to the "Ready to Install the Program" panel after it opens.
Go to the "Ready to Install the Program" panel after it opens. Select work-related or other programs that you want displayed as desktop shortcuts on your computer.
- During the installation process, the software will automatically place the PC Anywhere shortcut on your desktop.

6 - Choose "Install.
Choose "Install." Follow the prompts on the screen to complete the process. Select "Finish" when you are done.
- You may need to restart your computer to update files and ensure that PC Anywhere functions properly.

7 - Insert the PC Anywhere CD into your computer's disk drive.
Insert the PC Anywhere CD into your computer's disk drive. Wait for the installation panel window to open, and then press "View Other Installation Options."

8 - Select "View Custom Installation Packages" in the next menu.
Select "View Custom Installation Packages" in the next menu.

9 - Follow the software's instructions for customizing and finalizing the installation.
Follow the software's instructions for customizing and finalizing the installation. Pick "OK" to finish installing PC Anywhere.
