Lock Desktop Icons in Place
https://www.wikihow.com/Lock-Desktop-Icons-in-Place

0 - Sorting by Tags on Mac

1 - Right-click each desktop item.
Right-click each desktop item. This opens a drop-down menu. On a Mac with a trackpad or a magic mouse, click the desktop item with two fingers, or hold the ⌥ Option key and click to perform a right-click.

2 - Select a colored tag.
Select a colored tag.  Use the colored tags to organize the desktop items however you wish.  Icons will be arranged by color in the order they appear in the drop-down menu.

3 - Right-click an empty space on the desktop.
Right-click an empty space on the desktop. This opens a drop-down menu. On a Mac with a trackpad or a magic mouse, click the desktop item with two fingers, or hold the ⌥ Option key and click to perform a right-click.

4 - Click Sort By.
Click Sort By.

5 - Select Tags.
Select Tags. It's near the bottom of the pulldown menu. This will organize your desktop icons in the tag order you selected and they will then be locked in place.
