Fix Skype Microphone Problems on PC or Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Fix-Skype-Microphone-Problems-on-PC-or-Mac

0 - Using Windows

1 - Open Skype on your computer.
Open Skype on your computer. The Skype app looks like a white "S" in a blue circle on your Start menu.
- If you're not automatically logged in, tap the Sign In button, and sign in with your Skype Name, email or phone and your password.

2 - Click the Skype tab.
Click the Skype tab. This button is in the upper-left corner of the app window. It will open a drop-down menu.

3 - Hover over Profile on the Skype menu.
Hover over Profile on the Skype menu. A sub-menu will pop out.

4 - Click Change Sounds.
Click Change Sounds. This will open your sound settings in a new window.

5 - Click Audio settings on the left sidebar.
Click Audio settings on the left sidebar. This option is located above Sounds on the left-hand side.

6 - Select your microphone.
Select your microphone. Click the drop-down menu next to Microphone and select your default microphone.
- Here, a green bar indicates your microphone's sound input level. If the green bar is rising when you speak, your microphone is working.

7 - Click the blue Save button.
Click the blue Save button. This will save your new microphone settings.
