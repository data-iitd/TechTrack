Fix Skype Microphone Problems on PC or Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Fix-Skype-Microphone-Problems-on-PC-or-Mac

0 - Using Mac

1 - Open Skype on your computer.
Open Skype on your computer. The Skype icon looks like a white "S" in a blue circle in your Applications folder
- If you're not automatically logged in, click the Sign In button, and sign in with your Skype Name, email or phone and your password.
- ;

2 - Click the Skype tab.
Click the Skype tab. This button is on your Mac's menu bar in the upper-left corner of your screen. It will open a drop-down menu.

3 - Click Audio & Video Settings.
Click Audio & Video Settings. This will open your settings in a new window.

4 - Select your microphone.
Select your microphone. Click the drop-down menu next to Microphone and select your default microphone.

5 - Close the settings window.
Close the settings window. Click the "X" icon in the top left to close it. Your new microphone settings will be saved automatically.
