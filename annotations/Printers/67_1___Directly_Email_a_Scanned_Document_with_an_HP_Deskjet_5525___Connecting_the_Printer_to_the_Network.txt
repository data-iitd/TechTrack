0 - Connecting the Printer to the Network

1 - Tap "Settings.
Tap "Settings." This is the second icon on the menu of the control panel - the one with the satellite icon.

2 - Tap "Wireless Setup Wizard.
Tap "Wireless Setup Wizard." You may have to scroll down to find this.

3 - Select Network SSID.
Select Network SSID. From the list of available networks, select your home network.

4 - Enter your wireless passcode.
Enter your wireless passcode. Input your home network’s security key or passcode.

5 - Confirm settings.
Confirm settings. You will be asked to confirm your settings. Tap "Okay." The printer will try to establish a connection to your network.

6 - View confirmation.
View confirmation. A “Confirmation Successful” message will appear if the printer was able to connect successfully.
