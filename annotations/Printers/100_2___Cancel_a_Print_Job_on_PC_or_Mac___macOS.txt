2 - Click the System Preferences.
Click the System Preferences.

3 - Click Printers.
Click Printers. This may be called Printers & Scanners on some computers.

4 - Click Open Print Queue.
Click Open Print Queue. It’s next to the printer icon at the center of the window. A list of pending/queued print jobs will appear.

5 - Click the print job you want to cancel.
Click the print job you want to cancel. This selects the job.
- If your print job doesn’t show up here, this usually means the document has already printed.

6 - Click the Jobs menu.
Click the Jobs menu. It’s in the menu bar at the top of the screen.

7 - Click Delete job.
Click Delete job. It’s the first option. This removes the print job from the print queue.
