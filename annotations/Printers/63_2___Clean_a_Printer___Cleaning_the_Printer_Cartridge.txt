0 - Cleaning the Printer Cartridge

1 - Open your printer and remove the ink cartridge.
Open your printer and remove the ink cartridge. Locate your access panel, which will be either on the front or back of your printer, depending on the model. Following the directions in your manual, gently pull out the printer cartridge. They should just pop out pretty easily.[9]

- Research source

2 - Place the cartridge, nozzles down, into a bowl of warm water.
Place the cartridge, nozzles down, into a bowl of warm water. There will be small nozzles on one side of the cartridge. Submerge these nozzles in a bowl of warm water.[10]
- X
- Research source
- 
- The water should be just a little warmer than room temperature.

3 - Remove the cartridge when you see ink begin to flow in the water.
Remove the cartridge when you see ink begin to flow in the water. When the nozzles are clean, ink will be able to flow freely. This shouldn’t take long.

4 - Dry the cartridge with a clean cloth and let it sit for 10 minutes.
Dry the cartridge with a clean cloth and let it sit for 10 minutes. After you take the cartridge out of the bowl, wipe it dry with a clean cloth. Allow it to air dry for about 10 minutes.[11]

- Research source

5 - Replace the cartridge and print a test page.
Replace the cartridge and print a test page. Pop the cartridge back into the printer and close the access panel. Your printer will probably print a test page.[12]
