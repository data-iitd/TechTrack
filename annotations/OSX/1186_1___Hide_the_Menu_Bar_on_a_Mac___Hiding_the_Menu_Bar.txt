Hide the Menu Bar on a Mac

0 - Hiding the Menu Bar

1 - Click the Apple menu.
Click the Apple menu.
- ;

2 - Select "System Preferences."
Select "System Preferences."

3 - Click the "General" option.
Click the "General" option. If you don't see this, click the "Show All" button first, which looks like 12 dots arranged in a grid pattern at the top of the window.

4 - Check the "Automatically hide and show the menu bar" box.
Check the "Automatically hide and show the menu bar" box. This will hide the menu bar when the mouse is not over it.

5 - Move your mouse to the top of the screen.
Move your mouse to the top of the screen. This will reveal the menu bar.[1]
