2 - Open QuickTime on your Mac.
Open QuickTime on your Mac. It's the gray-and-blue "Q" icon on the Launchpad and in the Applications folder.

3 - Click the File menu.

4 - Click New Screen Recording on the menu.

5 - Select Internal Microphone from the menu.

6 - Click the red circle button.

7 - Drag the crosshairs over to select the video.

8 - Click Record and start playing the video.
Click Record and start playing the video. If the sound isn't on, be sure to turn it on now.
