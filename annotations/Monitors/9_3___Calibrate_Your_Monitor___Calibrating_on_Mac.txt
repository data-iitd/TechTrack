0 - Calibrating on Mac

1 - Open the Apple menu .
Open the Apple menu . Click the Apple logo in the top-left corner of the screen. A drop-down menu will appear.

2 - Click System Preferences….
Click System Preferences…. It's in the drop-down menu. Doing so opens the System Preferences window.

3 - Click Displays.
Click Displays. This option is in the System Preferences window. A pop-up window will open.

4 - Click Color.
Click Color. You'll find this tab at the top of the Displays window.

5 - Click Calibrate….
Click Calibrate…. It's on the right side of the page.

6 - Click Continue.
Click Continue. This option is in the bottom-right corner of the page.

7 - Follow the on-screen prompts.
Follow the on-screen prompts. Depending on your monitor, the options you see in the window may vary; however, in most cases, you'll just click Continue in the bottom-right corner of the page until you reach the password entry prompt.

8 - Enter your password when prompted.
Enter your password when prompted. Type the password that you use to log in into the "Password" text box, then click OK.

9 - Click Done when prompted.
Click Done when prompted. Doing so saves your monitor's calibration.
