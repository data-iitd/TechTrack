Change the Time Format on a Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Change-the-Time-Format-on-a-Mac

0 - Turning on 24-Hour Time

1 - Click the Apple menu.
Click the Apple menu.
- ;

2 - Click "System Preferences."
Click "System Preferences." If you are not taken to the main System Preferences menu, click the "Show All" button at the top of the window.

3 - Click "Language & Region."
Click "Language & Region." This icon can be found in the top row and has a flag for an icon.

4 - Click the "Time format" box.
Click the "Time format" box. This will toggle between 24-hour time and 12-hour time.

5 - Click the "Region" menu to change your country.
Click the "Region" menu to change your country. This will change your time and date formats automatically to match the country standard.
