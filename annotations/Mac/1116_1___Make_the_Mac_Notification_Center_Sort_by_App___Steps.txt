Make the Mac Notification Center Sort by App
https://www.wikihow.com/Make-the-Mac-Notification-Center-Sort-by-App

0 - Steps

1 - Click on the Apple menu.
Click on the Apple menu. It's the black, apple-shaped icon in the upper-left of your screen.
- ;

2 - Click on System Preferences.
Click on System Preferences.

3 - Click on Notifications.
Click on Notifications. It's in the upper-right of the preferences window.

4 - Click on the drop-down menu.
Click on the drop-down menu. It's at the bottom of the dialog box, next to  "Notification Center sort order."

5 - Click on the app sort order that you prefer.
Click on the app sort order that you prefer. Your Mac Notifications Center now sorts its notifications by app.
- "Recents by app" sorts all of the most recent notifications by app.
- "Manually by app" allows you to choose the order of notifications by dragging apps up or down in the list above the drop-down menu.
