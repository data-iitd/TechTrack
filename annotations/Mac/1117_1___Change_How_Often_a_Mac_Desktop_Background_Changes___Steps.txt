Change How Often a Mac Desktop Background Changes
https://www.wikihow.com/Change-How-Often-a-Mac-Desktop-Background-Changes

0 - Steps

1 - Click on the Apple menu.
Click on the Apple menu. It's the black, apple-shaped icon in the upper-left of your screen.
- ;

2 - Click on System Preferences.
Click on System Preferences.

3 - Click on Desktop & Screen Saver.
Click on Desktop & Screen Saver. It's near the top left of the Preferences window.

4 - Click on Desktop.
Click on Desktop. It's at the top of the dialog box.

5 - Click on the checkbox next to "Change picture."
Click on the checkbox next to "Change picture." Make sure it's blue and a checkmark is displayed.

6 - Click on the drop-down menu next to "Change picture."
Click on the drop-down menu next to "Change picture."

7 - Click on a frequency.
Click on a frequency. You can choose to have the desktop picture change every 5 seconds, every minute, every 5 minutes, every 15 minutes, every 30 minutes, every hour or every day. You've now changed the frequency of your Mac's desktop picture changes.
- You can also choose to have the picture change upon log-in or when the Mac wakes up from sleep.
