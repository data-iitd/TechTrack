Add a Keyboard Region to a Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Add-a-Keyboard-Region-to-a-Mac

0 - Steps

1 - Click on the Apple menu.
Click on the Apple menu. It's the black, apple-shaped icon in the upper-left of your screen.
- ;

2 - Click on System Preferences.
Click on System Preferences.

3 - Click on Keyboard.
Click on Keyboard. It's in the center of the preferences window.

4 - Click on Input Sources.
Click on Input Sources.

5 - Click on +.
Click on +. It's beneath the left pane of the dialog box.

6 - Click on the language_region you'd like to add.
Click on the language/region you'd like to add.
- You can type the region in the search box under the left pane of the dialog box to avoid scrolling through all available regions and languages.

7 - Click on Add.
Click on Add. You have now added a new keyboard region to your Mac.
