Change a Mac from American English to British English
https://www.wikihow.com/Change-a-Mac-from-American-English-to-British-English

0 - Steps

1 - Click on the Apple menu.
Click on the Apple menu. It's the black, apple-shaped icon in the upper-left of your screen.
- ;

2 - Click on System Preferences.
Click on System Preferences.

3 - Click on Language & Region.
Click on Language & Region.

4 - Click on +.
Click on +. It's below the "Preferred Languages" window on the left side of the dialog box.

5 - Click on English (U.K.).
Click on English (U.K.).

6 - Click on Add.
Click on Add.

7 - Click on Use English (U.K.).
Click on Use English (U.K.). British English is now the primary language on your Mac.
- If English (U.K.) already appears in the "Preferred Languages" window, simply drag it to the top of the list to make it your Mac's primary language.
