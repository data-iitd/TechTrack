Make the Calendar Show in the Mac Notification Center
https://www.wikihow.com/Make-the-Calendar-Show-in-the-Mac-Notification-Center

0 - Steps

1 - Click on the Apple icon.
Click on the Apple icon. It's the Apple logo in the top left corner of the menu bar.
- ;

2 - Click System Preferences.
Click System Preferences.

3 - Click the "Notifications" icon.
Click the "Notifications" icon. It looks like a grey square with a red dot in one corner.

4 - Click Calendar.
Click Calendar. It's located in the list on the left side of the window.

5 - Click on the "Show in Notification Center" check box.
Click on the "Show in Notification Center" check box. It's on the right side of the Notifications window.

6 - Click the red "x" button.
Click the red "x" button. Calendar will now be displayed in the Notification Center on your Mac.
