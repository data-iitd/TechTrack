0 - Steps

1 - Click on the Apple menu.
Click on the Apple menu. It's the black, apple-shaped icon in the upper-left of your screen.
- ;

2 - Click on System Preferences.
Click on System Preferences.

3 - Click on Spotlight.
Click on Spotlight. It's in the upper-right of the preferences window.
- If you don't see all of the System Preferences icons, click on ⋮⋮⋮⋮ in the top bar of the dialog box.

4 - Click on Search Results.
Click on Search Results.

5 - Check the box next to "Movies."
Check the box next to "Movies." It's listed alphabetically in the "Search Results" pane. Make sure it's blue and displaying a checkmark.

6 - Click on the Spotlight icon.
Click on the Spotlight icon. It's the black magnifying glass on the menu bar in the upper-right corner of your screen.

7 - Type the title of a movie into the search bar.
Type the title of a movie into the search bar. Results will appear in the left pane as you type.

8 - Click on the movie title.
Click on the movie title. It should appear under the "Movies" subheading in the left pane of the dialog box.
- If your Mac is set to U.K. English, the heading will be "Films."

9 - Scroll down to review information about the movie.
Scroll down to review information about the movie. Spotlight will display information about the movie, such as showtimes, rating and cast, in the right pane of the dialog box.
- To purchase tickets online, click on a displayed showtime. This will launch Fandango in your default web browser.
- To purchase or reserve a digital copy of the movie from iTunes, click on the Pre-Order/Buy from iTunes button at the top of the right pane.
