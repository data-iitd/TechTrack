Set a Keyboard Shortcut to Open Spotlight
https://www.wikihow.com/Set-a-Keyboard-Shortcut-to-Open-Spotlight

0 - Setting a Custom Keyboard Shortcut

1 - Click on the Apple menu.
Click on the Apple menu. It's the black, apple-shaped icon in the upper-left of your screen.

2 - Click on System Preferences.
Click on System Preferences.

3 - Click on Keyboard.
Click on Keyboard. It's in the center of the preferences window.

4 - Click on Shortcuts.
Click on Shortcuts.

5 - Click on Spotlight.
Click on Spotlight. It's in the left pane of the dialog box.

6 - Click on Show Spotlight Search.
Click on Show Spotlight Search. It's in the right pane of the dialog box. When you click on the text, it will highlight in blue.

7 - Click on ⌘Space.
Click on ⌘Space. It will highlight in lighter blue.

8 - Type the key combination for your custom shortcut.
Type the key combination for your custom shortcut. You have now set a keyboard shortcut for Spotlight.
- Return to this dialog box and click on Restore Defaults if you'd like to return the shortcuts to their default settings.
