Change the Measurement Units on a Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Change-the-Measurement-Units-on-a-Mac

0 - Steps

1 - Click on the Apple menu.
Click on the Apple menu. It's the black, apple-shaped icon in the upper-left of your screen.
- ;

2 - Click on System Preferences.
Click on System Preferences.

3 - Click on Language & Region.
Click on Language & Region.

4 - Click on the drop-down menu next to "Temperature."
Click on the drop-down menu next to "Temperature." It's on the right side of the dialog box. Select Fahrenheit or Celsius for units of temperature.

5 - Click on Advanced.
Click on Advanced.

6 - Click on the drop-down menu next to "Measurement Units."
Click on the drop-down menu next to "Measurement Units." Choose from U.S., U.K. or metric.

7 - Click on the drop-down menu next to "Currency."
Click on the drop-down menu next to "Currency." Select your preferred currency unit.

8 - Click on OK.
Click on OK. You've now changed your Mac's units of measurement to your preferred settings.
- Changing these settings will change the default units your Mac uses when it displays such information in places like the menu bar or the Notifications Center.
