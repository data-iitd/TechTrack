Use the Trackpad to Zoom on a Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Use-the-Trackpad-to-Zoom-on-a-Mac

0 - Enabling and Disabling Gestures

1 - Click the Apple menu.
Click the Apple menu. You can find this in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2 - Click System Preferences.
Click System Preferences.

3 - Click Trackpad.
Click Trackpad. If you don't see this option, click the Show All button at the top of the window.

4 - Click the Scroll & Zoom tab.
Click the Scroll & Zoom tab.

5 - Click the Zoom in and out box.
Click the Zoom in and out box. This will toggle the ability to pinch zoom on or off.

6 - Click the Smart zoom box.
Click the Smart zoom box. This will toggle the ability to double-tap two fingers to smart zoom on or off.
