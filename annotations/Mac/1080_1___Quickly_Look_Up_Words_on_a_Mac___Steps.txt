Quickly Look Up Words on a Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Quickly-Look-Up-Words-on-a-Mac

0 - Steps

1 - Click on the Apple menu.
Click on the Apple menu. It's the black, apple-shaped icon in the upper-left of your screen.
- ;

2 - Click on System Preferences.
Click on System Preferences.

3 - Click on Trackpad.
Click on Trackpad. It's in the center of the preferences window.

4 - Click on Point & Click.
Click on Point & Click. It's near the top of the dialog box.

5 - Click on the checkbox next to "Look up & data detectors."
Click on the checkbox next to "Look up & data detectors." Make sure the box is blue and contains a checkmark.

6 - Click on the drop-down below the checkbox.
Click on the drop-down below the checkbox. If your Mac is equipped with a Force Trackpad, you will have the option to select Force click. Otherwise, the default gesture is "Tap with three fingers."

7 - Highlight any word you wish to look up.
Highlight any word you wish to look up.

8 - Execute the Look Up gesture.
Execute the Look Up gesture. Either tap three fingers or Force Click on your trackpad. This will quickly give you dictionary entries for the word you want to look up.
