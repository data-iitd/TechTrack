0 - Zooming With Your Trackpad

1 - Open something that can be zoomed.
Open something that can be zoomed. You can change the zoom on web pages, pictures, documents, and more. You won't be able to zoom on the desktop or in Finder.
- ;

2 - Place two fingers on the trackpad.
Place two fingers on the trackpad.

3 - Move your two fingers apart to zoom in.
Move your two fingers apart to zoom in.

4 - Pinch your two fingers together to zoom out.
Pinch your two fingers together to zoom out.

5 - Double-tap two fingers to zoom in on your cursor.
Double-tap two fingers to zoom in on your cursor. Double-tap your two fingers again to zoom out. This will magnify the part of the document or web page that your cursor is over.[1]
