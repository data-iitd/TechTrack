Log Out of WeChat on PC or Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Log-Out-of-WeChat-on-PC-or-Mac

0 - Steps

1 - Open WeChat.
Open WeChat. It's the icon with two green overlapping speech bubbles. You should find it in the Windows menu (if you have a PC) or the Launchpad (if you're using a Mac).
- ;

2 - Click ☰.
Click ☰. It's at the bottom-left corner of the screen.

3 - Click Settings.
Click Settings. It's at the top-left corner of the window that appeared.

4 - Click Log Out.
Click Log Out. It's the button beneath your profile image.

5 - Click OK to confirm.
Click OK to confirm. The confirmation window reminds you that you won't be notified of new messages as long as you're logged out. If you prefer not to log out, just click Cancel. Otherwise, clicking OK will immediately lot you out of WeChat.
