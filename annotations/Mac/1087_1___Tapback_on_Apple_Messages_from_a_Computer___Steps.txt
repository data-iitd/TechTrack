Tapback on Apple Messages from a Computer
https://www.wikihow.com/Tapback-on-Apple-Messages-from-a-Computer

0 - Steps

1 - Make sure your Messages app is all set up to work on your computer.
Make sure your Messages app is all set up to work on your computer. If you don't have that working yet, you just need to activate iMessages on your desktop or laptop.
- ;

2 - Open Messages.
Open Messages.

3 - Locate the message you want to react to.
Locate the message you want to react to.

4 - Hit Ctrl and click the message.
Hit Ctrl and click the message. If you're using a laptop, you can use two fingers on the touchpad at once to "right-click." If you have a mouse with two buttons, you can also right-click directly on it.

5 - Select Tapback from the menu.
Select Tapback from the menu. You'll see a reaction menu pop up.

6 - Pick your reaction.
Pick your reaction. You'll have a choice between a heart, thumbs up, thumbs down, "Ha Ha," exclamation points, or question mark.
- As soon as you pick it, it'll send that reaction to your recipient. There's no need to hit send or click anything else.
