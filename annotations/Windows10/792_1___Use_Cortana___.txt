1 - Open Start .
Open Start . Click the Windows logo in the bottom-left corner of the screen.

2 - Open Settings .
Open Settings . Click the gear-shaped icon in the lower-left side of the Start window.

3 - Click Cortana.
Click Cortana. It's a circular icon at the bottom of the Settings page.

4 - Click the Talk to Cortana tab.
Click the Talk to Cortana tab. You'll see this in the upper-left side of the page.

5 - Click the grey "Let Cortana respond to 'Hey Cortana'" switch .
Click the grey "Let Cortana respond to 'Hey Cortana'" switch . It will change color . Cortana will now activate when you say "Hey, Cortana" near your computer.
- If you don't want to enable "Hey Cortana", you can still give Cortana vocal commands by clicking the microphone button in the bottom-right corner of the Cortana window when it's open.
- You can also set Cortana to respond to only your voice by clicking the Learn how I say "Hey Cortana" link and then following Cortana's prompts.
