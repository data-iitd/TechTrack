Turn Off Windows Defender in Windows 10
https://www.wikihow.com/Turn-Off-Windows-Defender-in-Windows-10

0 - Turning Off Windows Defender

1 - Open Start .
Open Start . Click the Windows logo in the bottom-left corner of the screen. The Start menu will pop up.
- ;

2 - Open Settings .
Open Settings . Click the gear-shaped Settings icon in the lower-left side of the Start menu. Doing so opens the Settings window.

3 - Click  Update & Security.
Click  Update & Security. It's in the bottom row of Settings options.

4 - Click Windows Security.
Click Windows Security. This tab is in the upper-left side of the window.

5 - Click Virus & threat protection.
Click Virus & threat protection. It's the first option below the "Protection areas" heading near the top of the page. Doing so opens the Windows Defender window.

6 - Click Virus & threat protection settings.
Click Virus & threat protection settings. You'll find this option in the middle of the page.

7 - Disable Windows Defender's real-time scanning.
Disable Windows Defender's real-time scanning. Click the blue "On" switch  below the "Real-time protection" heading, then click Yes when prompted. This will turn off the real-time scanning feature of Windows Defender.[1]
