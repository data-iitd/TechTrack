Make a Network Connection Private in Windows 10
https://www.wikihow.com/Make-a-Network-Connection-Private-in-Windows-10

0 - 

1 - Open the Settings app
Open the Settings app. Click the Start  button and select the settings  gear.

2 - Go to the Network & Internet category.
Go to the Network & Internet category.

3 - Go to your network type.
Go to your network type. Click either Wi-Fi or Ethernet from the left pane.

4 - Open the properties of the current connection.
Open the properties of the current connection. Click the name of the current network box in the center portion of the window.

5 - Make your PC discoverable.
Make your PC discoverable. Toggle on  the switch under the "Make this PC discoverable" header. Doing so will change your connection type to private.
