0 - Changing Your Microsoft Account Name

1 - Open Settings.
Open Settings. Click on the Windows icon on the left bottom, and then click on Settings.

2 - Click on Accounts.
Click on Accounts.

3 - Click on Your info in the left panel.
Click on Your info in the left panel. Then click on Manage my Microsoft account on the right panel.

4 - Sign into your Microsoft account in the browser.
Sign into your Microsoft account in the browser. Click on More actions, and then select Edit profile.

5 - Click on Edit name under your user account.
Click on Edit name under your user account.

6 - Type in your new user name.
Type in your new user name. Then click Save.

7 - Restart your PC to the logon screen.
Restart your PC to the logon screen. You will see your user account name has been changed.
