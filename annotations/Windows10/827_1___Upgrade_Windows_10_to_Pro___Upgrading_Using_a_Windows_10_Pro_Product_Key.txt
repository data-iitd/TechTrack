0 - Upgrading Using a Windows 10 Pro Product Key

1 - Confirm that your software is up-to-date.
Confirm that your software is up-to-date. To check, enter Updates in the search bar at the bottom of the screen, select Check for Updates. From there, you'll either see confirmation  that your system is up-to-date, or you'll be able to select Restart Now to begin the update.
- ;

2 - Click on the Start button .
Click on the Start button . This is the icon with for squares at the bottom left corner of your screen.

3 - Click on Settings .
Click on Settings .

4 - Click on Update and Security.
Click on Update and Security. You may have to scroll down a bit on your page to see this option.

5 - Click on Activation.
Click on Activation. You should find this option on the side panel of the pop up window.

6 - Click on Change product key.
Click on Change product key.

7 - Enter the 25-character Windows 10 Pro product key.
Enter the 25-character Windows 10 Pro product key.

8 - Select Next to start the upgrade to Windows 10 Pro.
Select Next to start the upgrade to Windows 10 Pro.

9 - Follow along with the prompts to finish installation.
Follow along with the prompts to finish installation. Your system may restart a number of times.
