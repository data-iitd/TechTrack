0 - Deleting a Painting from Fresh Paint

1 - Find the painting that you want to delete.
Find the painting that you want to delete. Scroll through the Fresh Paint homepage until you find the painting which you want to remove.
- If you saved the painting as a file, deleting it from Fresh Paint will not delete it from your computer.

2 - Right-click the painting.
Right-click the painting. Doing so will select it.

3 - Click ☰.
Click ☰. It's in the top-left corner of the window. A menu will appear.

4 - Click Delete.
Click Delete. This option is near the bottom of the menu.

5 - Click Delete when prompted.
Click Delete when prompted. Doing so removes the selected painting from Fresh Paint.
