0 - Clearing Internet History on Firefox

1 - Open Firefox.
Open Firefox. Click or double-click the Firefox app, which resembles a blue globe with an orange fox wrapped around it.

2 - Click ☰.
Click ☰. It's in the top-right corner of the page. A drop-down menu will appear.

3 - Click Library.
Click Library. This option is in the middle of the drop-down menu.

4 - Click History.
Click History. It's near the top of the menu.

5 - Click Clear Recent History….
Click Clear Recent History…. This is near the top of the menu. Clicking it opens a pop-up window.

6 - Select a time range to clear.
Select a time range to clear. Click the "Time range to clear" drop-down box, then click a time span (e.g., Last Hour).
- Selecting Everything in the drop-down menu will ensure that all of your history is cleared.

7 - Click the "Details" drop-down icon.
Click the "Details" drop-down icon. It's a downward-facing arrow to the left of the "Details" heading. A menu will appear.

8 - Check the "Browsing & Download History" box.
Check the "Browsing & Download History" box. You'll find this at the top of the menu.
- You can uncheck every other box in this menu if you please.

9 - Click Clear Now.
Click Clear Now. This option is at the bottom of the menu. Doing so clears your Firefox history for the selected time range.
