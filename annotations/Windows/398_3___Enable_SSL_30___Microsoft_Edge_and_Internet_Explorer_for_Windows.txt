0 - Microsoft Edge and Internet Explorer for Windows

1 - Press ⊞ Win+S.
Press ⊞ Win+S. This opens the search bar.

2 - Type internet options.
Type internet options. A list of matching results will appear.

3 - Click Internet Options.
Click Internet Options.

4 - Click the Advanced tab.
Click the Advanced tab. A list of check boxes will appear under the “Settings” header.

5 - Scroll down and check the box next to “SSL 3.0.
Scroll down and check the box next to “SSL 3.0.”

6 - Click Apply.
Click Apply. It's at the bottom of the window.

7 - Click OK.
Click OK. It's next to the Apply button.

8 - Restart your PC as prompted.
Restart your PC as prompted. Once Windows starts back up, Edge and Internet Explorer will both support SSL 3.0.
