Make the Show Desktop Icon in Windows Quick Launch Toolbar
https://www.wikihow.com/Make-the-Show-Desktop-Icon-in-Windows-Quick-Launch-Toolbar

0 - 

1 - Right-click the shortcut.
Right-click the shortcut. A drop-down menu will appear.
- If your mouse doesn't have a right-click button, click the right side of the mouse, or use two fingers to click the mouse.
- If your computer uses a trackpad instead of a mouse, use two fingers to tap the trackpad or press the bottom-right side of the trackpad.

2 - Click Properties.
Click Properties. It's at the bottom of the drop-down menu. This opens the Properties window.

3 - Click Change Icon….
Click Change Icon…. This option is at the bottom of the window. Doing so opens a pop-up window.
- If you don't see this option, make sure you're on the correct tab by clicking Shortcut at the top of the window.

4 - Click the "Show Desktop" icon.
Click the "Show Desktop" icon. It's a blue-and-white rectangle with a pencil on it. Clicking this icon selects it.

5 - Click OK on both open windows.
Click OK on both open windows. Doing so confirms your icon choice and closes the Properties window. Your "Show Desktop" shortcut should now resemble the shortcut from Windows XP.
