Add a Webcam to OBS on PC or Mac
https://www.wikihow.com/Add-a-Webcam-to-OBS-on-PC-or-Mac

0 - Steps

1 - Connect the webcam to your PC or Mac.
Connect the webcam to your PC or Mac. If you haven’t yet set up your webcam, see Set up a Webcam now.;

2 - Open OBS Studio.
Open OBS Studio. It’s in the All Apps area of the Start  menu in Windows, and the Applications folder in macOS.

3 - Click + under “Sources.
Click + under “Sources.” It’s the first icon at the bottom of the “Sources” box. A menu will appear.

4 - Click Video Capture Device.
Click Video Capture Device. It’s near the bottom of the menu. This opens the “Create/Select Source” window.

5 - Type a name for the webcam.
Type a name for the webcam. It goes into the “Create New” box at the top of the window.

6 - Click OK.
Click OK. The Properties menu will appear.

7 - Select your webcam from the “Device” drop-down menu.
Select your webcam from the “Device” drop-down menu. It’s the first menu in the window.

8 - Click OK.
Click OK. It’s at the bottom of the window. Your webcam is now added to OBS Studio.
