0 - Changing Windows Sound Settings

1 - Open .
Open . Click the Windows logo in the bottom-left corner of the screen. The Start menu will open.

2 - Type sound into Start.
Type sound into Start. Doing so will search your computer for the Sound settings app.

3 - Click Sound.
Click Sound. It's a speaker-shaped icon at the top of the Start window.

4 - Click your headset's name.
Click your headset's name. You'll find it in the middle of the window.

5 - Click Set Default.
Click Set Default. It's in the lower-right side of the window. Doing so sets your headset as the default sound output item to use when it is connected.

6 - Click the Recording tab.
Click the Recording tab. This is at the top of the Sound window.

7 - Click your headset's name.
Click your headset's name. It's in the middle of the window.

8 - Click Set Default.
Click Set Default. This sets your headset as the default sound input item (e.g., microphone) when it is connected to your PC.

9 - Click Apply, then click OK.
Click Apply, then click OK. Doing so saves your settings. You're now ready to begin using your headset with your computer.
